## 2025-03-31 - [Adding `sanitizeHtml` to unmemoized list items degrades performance]
**Learning:** In the `thatmuch` codebase, adding `sanitizeHtml` calls (which use DOMPurify and take ~0.8ms) directly into rendering cycles of list items like `ProjectCard` introduces severe, un-memoized rendering overhead that blocks the main thread during component reconciliation, contradicting performance optimization efforts.
**Action:** Always verify if a proposed security fix (like adding `sanitizeHtml`) inadvertently introduces a massive performance penalty if placed inside a `.map()`. Look for opportunities to memoize the call at a higher level, lazily evaluate it, or avoid it if the data is already guaranteed to be safe from the CMS, specifically when acting under a strictly performance-focused directive.
